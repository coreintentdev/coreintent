# Track 07 — Yıldız Toplayan (Star Gatherer)

**Lane:** Turkish × te reo MI pillar
**Tempo:** 102 BPM
**Mood:** anatolian-rock, mountain-wind, hopeful resolve

---

## Lesson note (what this track teaches)
Turkish is **agglutinative** — verbs and nouns stack suffixes (-yor, -du, -muş, -lar, -ım, -ı...) into single long words. Most Suno prompts written in English-syntax will mangle this. The lesson here: **commit to the language's morphology in the lyric, but keep the style block in English** so Suno parses style correctly while singing Turkish faithfully. Also pairs saz/bağlama (Anatolian) with rock kit — a real living genre (Anadolu rock), not a fusion novelty.

## Email 2 — The AI Stack (Day 2)

**Subject:** Why we use 3 AI models instead of 1
**Preview text:** Disagreement is the feature, not the bug.

---

Hey {first_name},

Most trading platforms use one AI model and call it "AI-powered."

We use three. And they argue with each other.

**The Pipeline:**

1. **Grok** (xAI) — Scans markets and social feeds in real-time. Fast. Catches signals first.
2. **Claude** (Anthropic) — Deep analysis. Questions everything. "Should we actually act on this?"
3. **Perplexity** — Checks the news. Macro events. Regulatory shifts. The context layer.

**The Mechanism:**

- 3/3 agree → Strong signal. Act with conviction.
- 2/3 agree → Moderate confidence. Smaller position.
- Disagreement → We wait. The most valuable signal is sometimes "don't trade."

That last point is key. Single-model platforms optimise for signal volume. We optimise for signal quality. The trade you DON'T take is often worth more than the one you do.

**Try it:** Type `status` in the terminal to see the engine in action.

[Open Terminal →]

— CoreIntent

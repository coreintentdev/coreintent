## Template 3 — AI/Tech Publication

**Subject:** Multi-model AI orchestration in production — case study

---

Hi {editor_name},

I'm running three competing AI models (Claude, Grok, Perplexity) in a production trading signal pipeline. They deliberately disagree with each other — and that disagreement is the most valuable output.

**The architecture:**
- Grok handles fast signal detection and social sentiment
- Claude runs deep risk analysis and questions every signal
- Perplexity provides real-time research and macro context
- A consensus mechanism determines signal strength based on agreement level

**The insight:** Model disagreement is more valuable than agreement. When Grok says "buy" and Claude says "the risk profile doesn't support this," that tension triggers a deeper analysis loop no single model would initiate.

Happy to write a technical guest post or provide an interview on multi-model AI orchestration in fintech.

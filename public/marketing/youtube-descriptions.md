## Video 2 — Technical Deep Dive: "How 3 AI Models Cross-Check Every Signal"

**Description:**

A technical walkthrough of CoreIntent's multi-model signal pipeline. We use three AI models that actively disagree with each other — and that disagreement is the most valuable output.

Layer 1: Grok (xAI) — fast signal detection and social sentiment analysis
Layer 2: Claude (Anthropic) — deep market reasoning and risk assessment
Layer 3: Perplexity — real-time research, news, and fundamental analysis

When all three agree: high-confidence signal.
When two agree: moderate confidence, reduced position sizing.
When they disagree: sit out. The most valuable signal is sometimes "wait."

Six specialised agents (TrendFollower, MeanRevert, SentimentBot, ArbitrageBot, RiskGuard, ResearchAgent) execute within this framework.

Tech stack: Next.js 15, TypeScript (strict), Vercel, Cloudflare Pro, Cloudzy VPS.

Currently paper trading — validating the architecture before deploying with real capital.

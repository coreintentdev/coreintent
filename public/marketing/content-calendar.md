## Content Strategy

**Posting cadence:**
- X/Twitter: 1-2 posts per day
- LinkedIn: 2-3 posts per week
- Instagram: 3-4 posts per week (mix of carousels, reels, static)
- TikTok: 2-3 videos per week

**Content pillars:**
1. **Product** — What CoreIntent is and how it works
2. **Philosophy** — Why competitions beat subscriptions
3. **Technical** — Multi-model AI architecture deep dives
4. **Transparency** — Build in public updates, honest labelling
5. **Founder** — Corey's NZ bootstrap story

**Best posting times (NZ timezone, adjust for audience):**
- X/Twitter: 8am NZST / 6pm NZST (catches US evening + APAC morning)
- LinkedIn: Tuesday-Thursday 9am NZST
- Instagram: Weekdays 12pm NZST, weekends 10am NZST
- TikTok: 7pm-9pm NZST

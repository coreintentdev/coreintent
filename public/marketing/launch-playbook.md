## Channel Strategy

### X/Twitter — Primary Channel

**Why:** Crypto and AI audiences live here. Thread culture rewards deep content.

**Tactics:**
- 1-2 posts per day minimum
- 1 thread per week (technical or philosophy)
- Quote-tweet engagement with crypto/AI influencers
- Reply-guy strategy on trending topics
- Weekly polls for engagement

**Content mix:** 40% product, 25% philosophy, 20% technical, 15% founder story

### LinkedIn — Professional Credibility

**Why:** Investor/partner audiences. Establishes Corey as a thought leader.

**Tactics:**
- 2-3 posts per week
- Long-form thought leadership
- Engagement in fintech and AI groups
- Connection requests to target audience

**Content mix:** 30% technical, 30% philosophy, 25% founder, 15% product

### TikTok — Viral Reach

**Why:** Short-form video drives massive top-of-funnel awareness.

**Tactics:**
- 2-3 videos per week
- Hook-driven scripts (first 3 seconds critical)
- Trending audio when relevant
- Screen recordings of the terminal

**Content mix:** 40% hooks/hot takes, 30% educational, 30% founder story

### Instagram — Visual Brand

**Why:** Carousels drive saves and shares. Visual platform for brand identity.

**Tactics:**
- 3-4 posts per week
- Carousel-heavy strategy (comparison, how-it-works, stats)
- Reels for short-form content
- Story polls and Q&As

**Content mix:** 35% carousels, 30% reels, 20% static, 15% stories

### Email — Owned Audience

**Why:** Only channel you fully own. Highest conversion rate.

**Tactics:**
- Weekly newsletter
- Terminal-style design (on-brand)
- Build in public updates
- Competition results when live

## Content Repurposing Framework

Every piece of content should be repurposed across at least 3 channels:

```
Twitter thread → LinkedIn article → TikTok script → Instagram carousel → Newsletter section
```

**Example flow:**
1. Write "Why 3 Models?" thread for X (Post 13)
2. Expand into LinkedIn long-form (Post 7)
3. Cut into TikTok script (Script 6 — "The Signal Pipeline")

## Measurement & Iteration

### Weekly Review

- Total impressions across all channels
- Engagement rate per post (likes + comments + shares / impressions)
- Website traffic from social referrals
- Email subscriber growth
- GitHub star growth

### Monthly Review

- Follower growth rate per channel
- Top 3 performing posts (what worked and why)
- Audience demographics shift
- Conversion: social follower → email subscriber → platform user
- Content pillar performance (which pillar drives most engagement?)

### Iteration Rules

1. Double down on what works — if transparency posts outperform, post more
2. Kill what doesn't — if TikTok underperforms after 30 days, reallocate effort
3. Test one new format per week — polls, threads, carousels, spaces
4. Always A/B test hooks — the first line determines everything

### Email 2: The AI Stack (Day 1)

**Subject:** How three AI models argue about your trade

**Body:**

Most trading tools use one model and hope it's right.

CoreIntent uses three — and the disagreement is the point.

**The Pipeline:**

1. **Grok** spots the signal first. Fast pattern recognition, social sentiment. The first responder.

2. **Claude** questions it. Deep analysis, risk assessment. "Should we actually act on this?"

3. **Perplexity** fact-checks against live data. "What does the broader context say?"

**The outcome:**
- All three agree → strong conviction signal
- Two agree → moderate confidence, proceed with caution
- All disagree → sit out, flag uncertainty

This isn't marginal improvement. It's a fundamentally different approach to signal quality.

Try it: open the terminal and type `brain` to see the AI orchestra.

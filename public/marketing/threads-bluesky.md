## Platform-Specific Notes

**Threads posting cadence:**
- 3-4 posts per week
- Best times: Early morning (catches US evening engagement)
- Longer, more conversational tone OK
- Reply to comments frequently — algorithm rewards interaction

**Bluesky posting cadence:**
- 4-5 posts per week
- Shorter, punchier — similar to early Twitter
- Tech-savvy audience appreciates technical depth
- Custom feeds (starter packs) for discovery
- Add relevant tags for Bluesky feed algorithms

**Cross-posting note:**
Don't copy-paste between platforms. Adapt tone:
- Threads = conversational, storytelling
- Bluesky = concise, technical, community-first
- Both = more authentic than X/Twitter (less "growth hacking" energy)

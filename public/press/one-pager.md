## The Problem

Trading platforms charge $50-$300/month in subscriptions whether the trader profits or not. The platform wins either way. That's not alignment — that's indifference with a dashboard.

## The Solution

**CoreIntent** — an agentic AI trading engine that kills the subscription model and replaces it with free competitions. Three AI models cross-checking every signal. Bots competing alongside humans. Infrastructure so lean it costs less than a round of drinks.

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Model** | Competition-based (daily / weekly / monthly leagues) |
| **Entry** | Free |
| **AI Stack** | Claude + Grok + Perplexity (multi-model orchestration) |
| **Agents** | 6 AI trading agents with distinct strategies |
| **Bots** | First-class — AI-to-AI competition is a core feature |
| **Status** | Paper trading mode (pre-live) |

## How It Works

```
Register -> Learn -> Compete -> Earn -> Create
```

1. **Register** — Humans and bots. No captcha, no barriers
2. **Learn** — AI-powered terminal, docs, and agent fleet (all free)
3. **Compete** — Daily/weekly/monthly leagues with real stakes
4. **Earn** — Win competitions, earn rewards and multipliers
5. **Create** — Build strategies, music (SongPal), content

## Technology

**Multi-model AI orchestration:**
- **Grok (xAI)** — Fast signal detection, social sentiment
- **Claude (Anthropic)** — Deep analysis, risk assessment, strategy validation
- **Perplexity** — Real-time research, news, fundamentals

**6 AI Agents:** TrendFollower, MeanRevert, SentimentBot, ArbitrageBot, RiskGuard, ResearchAgent

**Stack:** Next.js 14 / TypeScript (strict) / Vercel / Cloudflare Pro / Cloudzy VPS

**Monthly infrastructure cost:** ~NZ$45

## Market Opportunity

- **$12B+ global online trading platforms market** — growing rapidly with AI adoption
- **AI-powered trading tools** — high demand, fragmented market, most platforms still single-model
- **Competition/GameFi model** — proven engagement drivers in gaming (tournaments generate 10x retention vs subscriptions), novel application in trading
- **Bot-inclusive design** — institutional trading is 70%+ algorithmic; retail platforms blocking bots are fighting the wrong war
- **Multi-model orchestration** — the shift from single-model to multi-model AI creates a moat that subscription platforms can't easily replicate
